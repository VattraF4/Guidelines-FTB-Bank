1.Account

    Account

      Account option is used to see the account details of the user.It's provides details about your bank accounts, including balances, transaction history, and account management features.

    1. Account Balance: Shows the current and available balance for each account
    2. Account Management: Includes actions like renaming accounts, activating/deactivating, or linking them to specific services
    3. Account Details: Provides information about each account, such as account number, account type, and account status
    4. Transaction History: Lists all recent deposits, withdrawals, and transactions.

2. Card

    Card

      Card option is used to Allows you to manage your debit/credit cards, view card details, set spending limits, freeze/unfreeze cards, and report lost/stolen cards.

      1. Card Details: Displays your card number, expiration date, and usage limits.

      2. Spending Controls: Set daily or transaction-specific spending limits.

      3. Transaction Alerts: Enable notifications for transactions made using the card.

3. Cash Card

    Cash Card

      Covers prepaid or reloadable cards used for controlled spending:



    1. Reload Funds: Add money to the card from your bank account

    2. Transaction Monitoring: Check the card balance and transaction history

    3. Usage Limits: Set restictons for usage by category or amount

4. Transfer

    Transfer

      Facilitates fund transfers with ease:



    1. Transfer to Own FTB Account: Used to transfer funds to other FTB customers.

    2. Transfer to Other FTB Accounts: Send money to accounts in other banks using local or international transfer systems.

    3. Transfer to Other Banks: Used to send money to accounts overseas.

    4. Transfer to Card: allows users to transfer money directly to a debit or credit card.

    5. International Transfers: allows users to transfer money directly to a debit or credit card.

    6. Manage Beneficiary: allows users to manage the list of beneficiaries for easier and faster future transactions.

    7. Transfer in Schedule:allows users to set up scheduled or recurring transfers based on a specified date or frequency.

    8. Manage Favorites: This option is designed to save or remark specific accounts, cards, or services as "favorites" for quicker access during future transactions.

    9. Recently Transfer: provides users with a list of their most recent transfers for quick reference or repeat transactions.

5. Bill Payment

    Transfer

      Simplifies paying bills directly from your account:



    1. Pay Bills: This option allows users to pay various bills, such as utility bills or other invoices.

    2. Mobile Top-Up: This section is for adding credit to mobile phone accounts or purchasing mobile data.

    3. Payment History: This option enables users to view their past payment transactions.

    4. Scheduled Payments: This feature allows users to set up and manage payments that occur on a regular schedule.

    5. Show Favorites: This option likely displays the user's favorite or frequently used payment options for quick access.

6. Phone Top-up

      Top up your prepaid mobile phone balance instantly:



    1. Mobile Operators: Select from available mobile providers.

    2. Amount Selection: Choose or input the desired top-up amount.

    3. Quick Recharge: Save frequently used numbers for faster top-ups.

    4. Promotions: Check for discounts or special offers on top-ups.



7. QR Payment/Scan

    Scan QR

      The QR Scan/QR Payment feature is designed to enable fast and secure transactions without manually entering payment details.


    Feature:


      Scan-to-Pay

    • Users can scan a merchant's or recipient's QR code to make payments directly from their account.

    • Supports QR codes compliant with the KHQR Standard (used in Cambodia) and other global QR payment standards.

    For Merchants:

    FTB provides the KHQR Merchant service, allowing businesses to receive instant payments conveniently and efficiently. Merchants can generate dynamic QR codes to accept payments from customers using FTB Mobile, other bank accounts, and e-wallets within the KHQR network. This service supports both USD and KHR transactions, enhancing cash management and reducing the risks associated with handling physical cash


    For Individual customers:

    FTB Mobile app, individual users can perform QR code payments by scanning merchant QR codes for quick and secure transactions. The app supports payments via KHQR and QR Scan Cross Border, facilitating both domestic and international transactions. Additionally, users can pay bills by scanning QR codes without using cash, enhancing the convenience of cashless payments.
