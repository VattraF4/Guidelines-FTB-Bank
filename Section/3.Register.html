3. How to Register

Follow these steps to register for an account in the FTB Bank app:

Step1. Select language.

  • Open the FTB Bank app.
  • Select the language you want to use
    (English or Khmer).

Step 2: Register Your Account.

  • Click On(Register New User.)


Step 3: Register Your Account.

  • Click On(Proceed Using account number.)


Step4: Introduction.

  • Agree to the terms And conditions and continue.

Step5:Fill.


  • Fill in your account number And date of birth.

Step6: Register.

  • Fill code OTP

Step7: Create a UserName.

Create a username for your account.

  • Create Password

    • Password must be:

    • 6 digits or more.
    • Pure numbers.
    • Pure letters.
    • Mixed numbers,letters, or symbols.
      • After Click On (Continue).


Step8: Create PIN.


  • Create a PIN code Execute the asset.
  • 4 digits.
    • After Click On (Agree).
