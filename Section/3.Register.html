3. How to Register

    Follow these steps to register for an account in the FTB Bank app:

    1. Open the FTB Bank app.

  • Navigate to www.ftbbank.com (replace with the correct URL).
  • Register on Mobile app
    • After launching the app, you will be prompted to select your preferred language.
    • Options: English or Khmer.
    • Tap Next to proceed.
  • On the app's home screen, tap the "Register" option.
  • Fill in the required fields:
    • FTB Account Number: Input the account number linked to your FTB Bank account.
    • Mobile Number: Enter the phone number registered with your bank.
    • National ID/Passport Number: Provide the identification details you used to open the account.
    • Email Address: (If applicable) Enter your active email address for communication and verification.
  • Create your login credentials:
    • Username: Choose a unique username for accessing the app.
    • Password: Set a secure password with uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Accept the terms and conditions by checking the box.
