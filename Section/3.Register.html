3. How to Register

Follow these steps to register for an account in the FTB Bank app:

Part 1: Register Account

Step1. Select language.


  • Open the FTB Bank app.
  • Select the language you want to useEnglish or Khmer

  • Click On Become FTB client

Step2: Terms And conditions.


  • Please accept Terms and conditions.

  • Agree to Terms and Conditions: Carefully read the terms and conditions, then tap on the "Agree" button to proceed.



Step3: Fill In Your Details


  • Personal Details: Family name, Middle name, Nationality, Date of Birth, Place of Birth, Phone Number

  • Note: Ensure all information matches what FTB Bank has on file to avoid verification issues

  • Click On Continue

Step4:Verify Identity


  • Take and upload a clear photo of your National ID or passport. Ensure the text is legible.

  • Note: You can "Skip" to the next Step

  • Click On Confirm

Step5:Submit the Registration Request


  • Once all details are completed, submit your registration. You will receive a notification from the bank (via email or SMS) about the status.

  • Log in to Your Account After the bank verifies your information, you can log in and start using your account via the app or online banking.

Part 2: Login Account

Step1. Select language.


  • Open the FTB Bank app.
  • Select the language you want to use
    English or Khmer

Step 2: Activate New User.


  • Click On Activate New User
  • Click On Activate With Account number
  • Tap on the option to Activate With Account number


Step3: Terms And conditions.


  • Please accept Terms and conditions.
  • Agree to Terms and Conditions: Carefully read the terms and conditions, then tap on the "Agree" button to proceed.



Step4: Fill In Your Details.


  • Enter your Account Number.
  • Fill in your Date Of Birth
  • Submit Registration: Tap on "Register".
  • Enter OTP Code: Input the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered phone number via SMS.

Step5: Create Username and Password.

Choose a unique Username.

  • Set a Password with at least 6 characters. You can use a combination of:

    • Password must be:

    • 6 digits or more.
    • Pure numbers.
    • Pure letters.
    • Mixed numbers,letters, or symbols or Special characters (if desired).

    After Click On (Continue).


Step 6: Create PIN

  • Create a PIN code to execute the asset.
  • The PIN code must be 4 digits long.
  • After setting the PIN, click on "Agree".

Successful Registration: Congratulations! You have successfully registered.

Choose Security Questions (Optional):

  • Tap "Yes" to set up security questions for account recovery.
  • Or tap "No" to proceed without creating security questions.

Step7: Log In to Your Account:


  • Enter your newly created Username and Password to access your account.

  • Submit Login: Tap on "Login".

Your registration is complete, and you can now use FTB Mobile!
