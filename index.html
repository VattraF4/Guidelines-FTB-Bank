About FTB Bank

Foreign Trade Bank of Cambodia (FTB) Company Profile


The Bank was created following Sub-decree No. 1213 dated 10 October 1979 under the former regime of the State of Cambodia. In 2000, the Bank was separated from the direct management of the National Bank of Cambodia (“The Central Bank”) in order to transform its permanent identity into a state-owned commercial bank in Cambodia with the features of a Public Economic Enterprise and with the intention of conducting the autonomous business operation. In January 2001, the Bank obtained its banking license from the Central Bank to operate as a commercial bank in the Kingdom of Cambodia for a period of three years starting from 4 January 2001. This was the first license granted to enable the Bank to operate as a separate legal entity independent from the Central Bank's direct management. On 8 January 2004, the Bank obtained its 2nd banking license from the Central Bank for a period of three years starting from that date. The Bank now holds a commercial banking license, which was renewed for an indefinite period, on 21 December 2006. FTB is Cambodia's first and foremost bank. As Cambodia's private commercial bank, FTB has been providing customers with safe and reliable banking services for almost 40 years.

FTB Mobile

With FTB Mobile, customers can access financial services via FTB Mobile anytime and anywhere.

FTB Mobile is the new mobile app of Foreign Trade Bank of Cambodia. FTB mobile operates on both Android and iOS devices. Customers can access financial services via FTB Mobile anytime and anywhere.


Features of FTB Mobile

  • Balance inquiries
  • Fund transfers to local and international banks
  • Bill payment scheduling
  • QR code payments for goods and services
  • Loan applications
  • 24/7 customer support

Advantages of Using FTB Mobile

  • Convenience: Access banking services anytime, anywhere.
  • Security: Advanced technology ensures safe transactions.
  • Efficiency: Manage your banking on the go and save time.
  • Customer-Centric: Responsive support for all your inquiries.
  • Innovation: Modern features tailored to user needs.

Why Choose FTB Mobile?

FTB Mobile is crafted for fast, secure, and convenient banking. It combines cutting-edge technology with personalized support to provide the best experience for customers. It's your reliable partner for seamless financial management.

2. Download and Install

Once the app is downloaded, it will install automatically on your device. If prompted, allow permissions for the app to work correctly.

  1. Go to the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. Search for "FTB Bank".
  3. Click "Download" or "Install".

Once the app is downloaded, it will install automatically on your device. If prompted, allow permissions for the app to work correctly.

3. How to Register

  • 1. Open the FTB Bank app.
  • 2. Click on the "Register" button.
  • 3. Click on "Register".
  • 4. Fill in your details, such as phone number, email, and account details.
  • 4. Follow the instructions to verify your account.
